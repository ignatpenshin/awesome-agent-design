# About the Author

## Ignat Penshin

Ignat Penshin is an AI/ML engineer and systems architect specializing in large language model applications, multi-agent systems, and production AI infrastructure.

His work focuses on the intersection of distributed systems engineering and modern AI — designing scalable, reliable agent architectures that operate in production environments.

This book is the result of extensive research, hands-on implementation, and a deep commitment to making agentic AI accessible and practical for engineers building the next generation of intelligent systems.
